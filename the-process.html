The Process

Every step is clear and guided. No stress, just an effortless creative flow.

You’ll send me a quick message sharing what you have in mind: your goals, preferences, and any examples you love. I’ll personally reply to understand your vision and confirm if we’re a good fit to work together. Once you’re ready to begin, we’ll secure your project with a 50% initial payment and set your start date.
Once your project is confirmed, I’ll walk you through the full process: what I’ll handle, what I’ll need from you, and how we’ll stay organized along the way. You’ll have gentle guidance on structure, content, and visuals, keeping everything clear and easy from start to finish.
This is your space to bring your story to life: your words, your visuals, your essence. You can send everything as we move through the process, and I’ll be here to guide and refine it, making sure it feels professional yet true to you.
Here I begin shaping your website’s look and flow: colors, typography, spacing, and structure. You’ll receive a first preview so you can visualize how your message will take form online. Together, we’ll fine-tune every detail before the build begins.
Your website comes to life: responsive, animated, and ready to impress. You’ll have time to review the full version and share feedback. Once approved, you complete the remaining 50% payment, and your site is officially ready to launch into the world.
