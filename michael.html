Project Michael Thompson

Challenge: The challenge was to create a professional and approachable website for a fictional therapist, Michael Thompson, with a focus on anxiety, stress management, relationships, and personal growth. The goal was to design a platform that inspires trust and communicates his areas of expertise clearly. As a portfolio project, it also needed to highlight my ability to build service-based websites that balance authority with empathy

Solution: I designed a modern, structured layout with a calm and reassuring visual style. The color palette and typography were chosen to reflect clarity and professionalism, while the content sections guide visitors naturally through Michael’s key focus areas. Smooth navigation, subtle animations, and responsive design ensure that the website is engaging and accessible across devices.

Impact This project demonstrates how design choices can support therapists in presenting their services with credibility and warmth. The final result balances clear communication with emotional appeal, showing potential clients that Michael offers both professionalism and understanding. As part of my portfolio, it illustrates my ability to create impactful websites for professionals in mental health and personal development.
