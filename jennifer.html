Project Jennifer Williams

Challenge: For Dr. Jennifer Williams, a fictional psychologist, the challenge was to design a website that feels both professional and approachable. It needed to convey calm and trust while presenting services in a clear, credible way. As a portfolio project, it also had to highlight my ability to combine aesthetics with usability.

Solution: I created a clean, minimalist layout with warm neutral tones to set a welcoming atmosphere. The typography balances professionalism with approachability, while the structure guides visitors naturally through the hero, about, and services sections. Subtle animations add a modern touch without distraction, and the fully responsive design ensures a seamless experience across devices.

Impact: The final concept shows how thoughtful design choices can strengthen a professional’s online presence. It conveys trust and clarity, giving potential clients confidence before the first contact. For my portfolio, it serves as a strong example of how strategic structure and user-centered design can elevate service-oriented websites.
